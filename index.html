Butter Loved Cookies

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 cup chocolate chips
  • 4-5 Eggs
  • 2/4 cup Milk

Detailed Recipe

  1. Preheat the oven to 350.
  2. Cream the butter and suger in a bowl.
  3. Add the dry ingredients and chocolate chips.
  4. Drop spoonfuls of dough into a baking sheets.
  5. Bake for 10-20 minutes until golden brown.
  6. Cool on a wire rack and enjoy!
